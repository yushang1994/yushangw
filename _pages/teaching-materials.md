    * <span style="font-size:16px;"> Official U.S. data on GDP, national income, consumption, and investment </span> <br>
  * <span style="font-size:16px;"> [U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS)](https://www.bls.gov) </span> <br>
    * <span style="font-size:16px;"> Employment, unemployment, inflation (CPI, PPI), and wage statistics </span> <br>
  * <span style="font-size:16px;"> [U.S. Census Bureau](https://www.census.gov) </span> <br>
    * <span style="font-size:16px;"> Demographics, income, population, and economic surveys </span> <br>
